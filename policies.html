Financing

Base price: $3.49 per miles

After 2,000 Miles: $4.49 per mile

After 5,000 Miles: $5.49 per mile

After 10,000 Miles: $6.49 per mile

Nautical Miles will be converted to Miles

Delivery

Our domestic fleet of trucks will pick up the food anywhere in the United States of America.

The Trucks will then go to Los Angles or Houston where the food will be loaded on ships.

These ships then go to the closest port to your intended destionation.

Our trucks will be waiting to then load the food and then move to deliver the goods

Return

Our cancelation policy only allows for the Product to be stopped while still in the United States.

Once cancelled our trucks will return the food to any desginated location you supply.

The miles will be charged for however far the truck has gone and takes to return.
